A North East based tech meetup

NE Dev is the quarterly north east developer meetup, based in Middlesborough which brings developers, engineers and designers together to share, learn and be inspired by each other.

NEDEV v3.0

Main Talk #1

NoSQL - a no brainer? - Glynn Bird

An introduction to NoSQL databases. Are they a good fit for your application or are you jumping on the buzzword bandwagon? Are all NoSQL databases the same, if not what are the pros and cons of each product. An insider's look into the loosely related world that is "NoSQL".

Glynn is an experienced developer who began his career in the field of Research and Development, programming Control & Instrumentation for the Steel industry. He then moved on to web development creating database, business directory and search applications primarily using SQL (PHP & ElasticSearch) and latterly using NoSQL (Node.js & Cloudant).

Main talk #2

Open Source Herbert - Jason Agnew

Going open-source can be rewarding, but working with the rest of the world creates some interesting challenges. The story of ‘Herbert’ is one of lessons learned, obstacles overcome, and how the biggest projects don’t always use the best practices

Jason is the Technical Director at Big Bite Creative, building and overseeing original web development projects for global clients.

Short Talk #1

The cloud and securing your assets - Marcus Dempsey

You’ve migrated your infrastructure to the cloud, your reaping the benefits of flexibility, lower costs, but how secure are you and your data?

Marcus has been in the IT industry for over 24 years, initially starting out as a programmer, then moving onto infrastructure and recently integrating the fundamentals of cyber security and infrastructure as a package. When not at work he has an interest in formula one, rock climbing and scuba diving.

Short Talk #2

Fun and Games with autoscaling - David Flower
