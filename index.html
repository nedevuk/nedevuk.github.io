A North East based tech meetup

NE Dev is the quarterly north east developer meetup, based in Middlesborough which brings developers, engineers and designers together to share, learn and be inspired by each other.

NEDEV v2.0

Main Talk #1

The UX Approach at Shop Direct - Lorna Kenwright

How one of the UK’s biggest online retailers implemented a UX lab from scratch, and evolved their design process.

Shop Direct, one on the UK’s leading online retailers with brands such as very.co.uk, littlewoods.com and isme.com.

Lorna Kenwright is a Senior Designer with the User Experience Team with over 10 years industry experience, covering all digital platforms.

Main talk #2

Why DevOps is like Dairy Farming - Ewan Steele

What is this DevOps thing anyway and why should we do it? Plus some of the common pitfalls and misconceptions...

Ewan is a devops engineer @Amplience, a Teesside University graduate and has over 8 years experience in IT.

Short Talk #1

Build your own heroku in 5 minutes - Jim Allanson

A rapid live-coding guide to setting up your own lightweight, low-cost heroku compatible PaaS in 600 seconds or less.

Jim is a front end engineer @Amplience, focusing on AngularJS and dabbling in full-stack.

Short Talk #2

Ansible - Configure & Deploy Servers Like a Pro - Matt Kirwan

This 5 minute crash talk will introduce you to Ansible - the incredibly powerful automation tool. You will leave this talk able to immediately apply Ansible into your infrastructure workflow, we will cover some of the more advanced features and above all be comfortable in starting to plot the demise of your system administrators.

Short Talk #3

5 Tips for dealing with scale - Darren Lee

25 billion requests a month and still humming....

Plus free pizza and drinks, thanks once again to our kind sponsor : Northstar Ventures
